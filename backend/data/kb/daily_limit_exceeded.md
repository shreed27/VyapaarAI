# Daily Transaction Limit Exceeded

## Error codes: U66, LIMIT_EXCEEDED, DAILY_LIMIT

## What happened
You have reached your bank's daily UPI transaction limit. No money was deducted.

## Daily limits by bank (approximate)
- Most banks: Rs 1,00,000 per day for peer-to-peer
- SBI: Rs 1,00,000 per day
- HDFC: Rs 1,00,000 per day (Rs 2,00,000 for business accounts)
- ICICI: Rs 1,00,000 per day
- Per transaction limit: typically Rs 10,000-25,000 for new accounts

## Resolution steps
1. Wait until midnight — limits reset at 12:00 AM
2. Use a different bank account linked to Paytm
3. For higher limits: upgrade your account at your bank branch
4. For business payments: use IMPS or NEFT for large amounts

## Prevention
Track your daily UPI usage in your bank app
