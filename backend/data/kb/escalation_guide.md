# UPI Escalation Guide

## When to escalate
Escalate when money was deducted but not received, a refund is overdue, settlement is delayed beyond the stated timeline, or fraud is suspected.

## Information to keep ready
- Transaction ID from Paytm
- UTR / RRN number
- Date and exact time of payment
- Amount
- Screenshot of payment status
- Recipient or merchant details

## Escalation path
1. Paytm app → Help → select the transaction → choose the relevant issue
2. If unresolved, contact the linked bank's customer care and provide the UTR
3. For merchant settlement issues: use Paytm for Business support
4. For suspected fraud: report immediately at cybercrime.gov.in and call 1930

## Expected investigation windows
- Simple status mismatch: 2-4 hours
- Failed/pending debit disputes: 2-5 business days
- Refund delays: 2-7 business days depending on payment rail
- Fraud complaints: act immediately, ideally within minutes

## Best practice
Escalate with evidence first instead of retrying the same transaction multiple times. Repeated retries can create duplicate-payment confusion.
