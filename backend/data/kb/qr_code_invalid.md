# Invalid or Unsupported QR Code

## Error codes: INVALID_QR, QR_PARSE_ERROR, UNSUPPORTED_QR

## What happened
The scanned QR code could not be read correctly or does not contain valid payment information for UPI.

## Why it happens
- QR code is damaged, blurry, or partially covered
- Screenshot quality is too low
- Merchant is using an outdated or unsupported QR format
- Printed QR points to a disabled VPA

## Resolution steps
1. Ask for a fresh printed QR or a digital QR shared directly from the merchant app
2. Increase screen brightness or improve camera focus before rescanning
3. If urgent: ask the merchant for their UPI ID manually
4. Merchant should regenerate the QR in Paytm for Business if repeated failures occur

## Prevention
Replace faded printed QR stickers and avoid photocopies or compressed screenshots
