# Merchant Settlement Delayed

## What happened
Your Paytm merchant settlement has not arrived on the expected date.

## Standard settlement timeline
- Normal transactions: T+1 business day (next working day)
- Disputed transactions: held until dispute resolved
- First settlement for new merchants: may take 3-5 business days

## Why delays happen
- Bank holiday: settlements skip non-working days
- Disputed transaction holding funds
- KYC issue with merchant account
- Technical processing delay at Paytm

## Resolution steps
1. Check expected settlement amount in Paytm for Business app
2. Verify no transactions are under dispute
3. Confirm your bank account details are correct in Paytm for Business settings
4. If delayed more than 2 business days: Contact Paytm Business support via app
5. Keep UTR numbers of the transactions ready when contacting support

## For urgent cases
Paytm Business support: 0120-4456-456 (business hours)
