# UPI PIN Blocked

## Error codes: U17, PIN_BLOCKED, MAX_PIN_ATTEMPTS

## What happened
Your UPI PIN has been temporarily blocked after multiple incorrect attempts. Until the lock is cleared, you cannot authorize UPI payments from this account.

## Why it happens
- Entered wrong UPI PIN three or more times
- Confused ATM PIN with UPI PIN
- Shared phone with someone who attempted payment using the wrong PIN

## Resolution steps
1. Wait for the temporary lock period to expire if your bank mentions a time window
2. Reset UPI PIN in Paytm app using debit card details and OTP
3. If reset fails: contact your bank and confirm the mobile number linked to the account is active
4. Do not keep retrying random PINs — that can extend the block

## Important note
UPI PIN is different from ATM PIN for many users. Always use the payment authorization PIN set for UPI.
