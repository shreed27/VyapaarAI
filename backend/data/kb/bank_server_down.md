# Bank Server Unavailable

## Error codes: U16, DEEMED_DECLINED, BANK_SERVER_DOWN

## What happened
Your bank's UPI server is temporarily unavailable. Paytm could not connect to your bank to process the payment. No money was deducted.

## Common affected banks and patterns
- SBI: frequent issues on weekends and after 10 PM
- HDFC: maintenance windows typically 11 PM - 12 AM
- ICICI: occasional slowdowns during salary processing days (1st and 7th of month)
- Axis: intermittent issues during peak load
- Kotak: generally stable but affected by national network issues

## Resolution steps
1. Confirm: money was NOT deducted (transaction never reached bank)
2. Try again after 15-30 minutes
3. If urgent: use a different bank account linked to Paytm
4. Check bank's official app/website for maintenance announcements

## Expected resolution
Bank servers typically recover within 30-60 minutes
