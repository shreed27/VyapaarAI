# Merchant Account Inactive

## Error codes: MERCHANT_INACTIVE, VPA_DISABLED, ACCEPTOR_BLOCKED

## What happened
The merchant's receiving UPI account is inactive, disabled, or temporarily not allowed to accept payments. The transaction was rejected before completion.

## Why it happens
- Merchant disabled their UPI ID or changed settlement account
- Merchant KYC or onboarding status is incomplete
- Risk or compliance hold placed on the merchant profile
- Old QR code is still displayed after account changes

## Resolution steps
1. Ask the merchant to check Paytm for Business app for account or KYC alerts
2. Request a fresh QR code from the merchant
3. If available, try the merchant's alternate payment method
4. Merchant should contact Paytm Business support if the account should be active

## For merchants
Replace printed QR codes immediately after any settlement account or UPI ID change
