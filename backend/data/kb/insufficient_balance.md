# Insufficient Balance

## Error codes: U51, INSUFFICIENT_FUNDS, LOW_BALANCE

## What happened
Your bank account did not have enough available balance to complete the UPI payment. The transaction was declined and no new deduction was made.

## Why it happens
- Available balance is lower than the payment amount
- A lien amount or minimum balance requirement reduced usable funds
- Recent debit card or ATM transaction has not yet reflected correctly in available balance
- Auto-debits or EMI deductions happened earlier the same day

## Resolution steps
1. Check your available balance in the bank app, not just the ledger balance
2. Reduce the payment amount or split the payment into smaller parts if appropriate
3. Add money to the account and retry after the balance refreshes
4. If the balance looks sufficient but payment still fails: try again after 10-15 minutes or contact the bank

## Prevention
Keep a small buffer balance in your primary UPI-linked bank account for frequent merchant payments
