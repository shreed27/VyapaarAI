# Collect Request Scam Warning

## What is a collect request
A collect request is when someone sends you a payment request asking YOU to approve sending money TO THEM. This is the opposite of receiving money.

## How the scam works
1. Scammer calls pretending to be from bank/Paytm customer care
2. Tells you "we will send you money, please approve this request"
3. Sends a collect request
4. Victim approves thinking they are RECEIVING money
5. Victim actually SENDS money to scammer

## Red flags — ALWAYS reject if:
- Request came from an unknown phone number
- Caller said "approve this to receive money" — legitimate payments never need approval from receiver
- Amount is a round number (500, 1000, 2000, 5000)
- Request came at unusual hours (before 8 AM or after 10 PM)
- Caller said they are from bank/Paytm/government

## What to do
1. REJECT the collect request immediately
2. Block the sender's number
3. Report to Paytm: Help → Report Fraud
4. Report to cybercrime: cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930

## Remember
Legitimate refunds and money transfers NEVER require you to approve anything. Money coming TO you arrives automatically.
