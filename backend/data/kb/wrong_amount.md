# Wrong Amount Transferred

## Sent more or less than intended

## If you sent the wrong amount to a merchant
1. Merchants cannot refund via UPI directly — contact the merchant in person
2. Merchant can initiate a refund via their Paytm for Business app
3. Refund takes 2-3 business days to credit back

## If you sent money to the wrong person
1. Immediately contact the recipient and request they return the money
2. Paytm cannot forcibly reverse a completed UPI transaction
3. If recipient refuses: file a complaint at cybercrime.gov.in
4. Keep screenshot of the transaction as evidence
5. Your bank can raise a chargeback request — contact bank within 24 hours

## If merchant received wrong amount
1. Contact customer immediately to pay the difference
2. Or issue a refund for the full amount and request correct payment
3. Always show customers the payment amount on Soundbox before they scan
