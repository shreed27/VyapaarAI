# Account Blocked / KYC Required

## Error codes: KYC_REQUIRED, ACCOUNT_BLOCKED, INACTIVE_VPA

## What happened
Your UPI account has been temporarily blocked due to incomplete KYC verification or suspicious activity detection by your bank.

## Why it happens
- KYC documents not submitted or expired
- Bank detected unusual activity (too many failed transactions, large sudden amounts)
- NPCI fraud flag triggered
- Bank account dormant for over a year

## Resolution steps
1. Check if your Paytm account shows a KYC alert in the app
2. Complete KYC: Paytm app → Profile → Complete KYC
3. For bank-level block: visit your bank branch with Aadhaar and PAN
4. For NPCI flag: contact Paytm support — they can help raise it with NPCI

## For merchants
A blocked UPI account means customers cannot pay you. This is urgent — visit bank branch same day if possible.
