# Refund Not Received

## What happened
You were told a refund was initiated but the money has not appeared in your account.

## Standard refund timelines
- UPI to UPI: 2-3 business days
- UPI to bank account: 5-7 business days
- Credit card refund: 7-10 business days
- Wallet refund: immediate to 24 hours

## How to track your refund
1. Paytm app → Help → Track Refund
2. Enter your transaction ID or order ID
3. Check the refund UTR number if one was provided

## If refund is overdue
1. Paytm app → Help → I didn't receive my refund
2. Provide: original transaction ID, amount, date, and refund UTR if available
3. Paytm support will investigate within 48 hours

## Bank processing
Even after Paytm initiates the refund, your bank takes 2-3 additional days to credit it. The refund UTR confirms Paytm's side is complete — remaining delay is at the bank.
